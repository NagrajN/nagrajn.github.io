We all pay taxes. The question we want to answer is, what is the share of federal taxes & what has changed over the decades.

There are four entities for the purposes of federal taxation:

Individuals are working people who pay the largest share of federal taxes. Note that this does not include social security taxes.

Corporations are US based business entities. These typically pay the second largest share of federal taxes.

Imports are tarriffs on goods coming into the US.

Foreign Entities are businesses or expats who earn outside the US but are still subject to federal tax law. These have the smallest share.
